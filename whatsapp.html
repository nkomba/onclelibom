Good to know

Questions, answered

How often will I receive messages?

One short devotional each day. No spam, and you can leave at any time.

Can I share the devotionals?

Yes — please do. Forwarding to friends, family, and church groups is warmly encouraged.

Which language will I get?

Whichever you choose — French, English, or both. Tell us when you join.

How do I stop?

Simply reply STOP / STOP and you will be removed. No questions asked.
