Home · Leadership Formation
For tomorrow's leaders

Preparing leaders the Church can trust

If the future of the Church is to flourish in Africa and beyond, it must be entrusted to leaders formed in Scripture, seasoned in character, and shaped by service.

Discipleship in Scripture

Formation begins with the Word. Emerging leaders are grounded in the whole counsel of Scripture, learning to feed themselves before they feed others.

Pastoral mentoring

No one leads well alone. Through accompaniment and honest counsel, seasoned leaders walk alongside those coming after them.

Ethical formation

Integrity in private is the root of authority in public. Leaders are formed to be trustworthy in church life, in the home, and in society.

Africa-rooted, globally relevant

A leadership vision that honours African wisdom and speaks to the whole Church — confident, humble, and ready to serve across cultures.

“And the things that you have heard from me among many witnesses, commit these to faithful men who will be able to teach others also.”
2 Timothy 2:2
The vision

A generation that acts on what it believes

“We must work to ensure that what we say is real. We cannot brag and not be acting.” This conviction of Rev. Dr. Jean Libom Li Likeng anchors Oncle Libom's approach to leadership: not titles, but faithfulness; not ambition, but service that endures and multiplies.
